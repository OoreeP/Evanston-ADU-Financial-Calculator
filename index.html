Evanston ADU Rules Overview

  • Max Size: Detached ADUs must be less than 1,000 sq ft. Interior conversions are exempt from this limit if on a single floor.
  • Max Height: Up to 28 feet for sloped roofs, and 20 feet for flat roofs.
  • Parking: No extra parking required for an ADU. Existing off-street parking must be maintained.
  • Occupancy: The City of Evanston does not mandate owner occupancy for the primary structure.
Source: Evanston 2020 Zoning Ordinance Updates.
